# 84 Validation

## Must-Pass Stories

1. Artifact to dataset:
   harvest a regression slice from a saved comparison, review it, promote it, and confirm it
   appears in the dataset catalog.
2. Dataset to rerun:
   rerun the promoted dataset with two deterministic fixture models and build reports for both.
3. Rerun to insight:
   compare those reruns with `--explain` and summarize the promoted dataset through `query
   --summarize`.
