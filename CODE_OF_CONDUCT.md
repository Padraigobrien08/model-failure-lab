# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community.

Expected behavior:

- Be constructive and kind.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable technical feedback.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Personal attacks or intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or ban participants for violations.
